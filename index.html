About Amanda

I am a sixth year graduate student in mathematics at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. My field of study is commutative algebra, and I am working under the direction of Srikanth Iyengar. In particular, I am interested in homological algebra, maximal Cohen-Macaulay modules, and Gorenstein rings.

My non-math hobbies include biking, traveling, reading, and playing with my cats.
